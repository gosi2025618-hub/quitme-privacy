"QuitMe"Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Overview

This app respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data from users.

You can use the app without creating an account and without providing any personal information.

2. Data Collection

This app does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (such as name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising data

3. Network Access

The app accesses the internet only to retrieve content such as:

  • Daily quotes
  • Daily words
  • Online dictionary content

No user data is uploaded during these requests.

4. Local Storage & Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage access: to save images generated by the app to your device
  • Sound: to play in-app sound effects
  • Vibration: to provide tactile feedback

All data generated by the app is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

5. Ads & Analytics

This app does not contain advertisements and does not use any analytics or tracking services.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app does not target children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Since the app does not collect any user data, no special data handling is required for children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be published on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer via email:

Email: jielemeapp@163.com

本应用高度重视用户隐私，不会收集、存储、共享或传输任何用户的个人信息。

使用本应用无需注册账号，也无需提供任何个人数据。

二、信息收集

本应用不会收集以下任何信息：

  • 个人身份信息（如姓名、邮箱、手机号）
  • 位置信息
  • 设备标识符
  • 使用行为数据
  • 广告或追踪数据

三、网络访问说明

应用仅在必要时访问网络，用于获取以下内容：

  • 每日金句
  • 每日一词
  • 在线词典内容

在此过程中不会上传任何用户数据。

四、本地存储与权限说明

应用可能请求以下权限：

所有内容仅保存在用户设备本地，不会上传至任何服务器。

五、广告与统计

本应用不包含任何广告，也不使用任何数据统计或分析工具。

六、儿童隐私
